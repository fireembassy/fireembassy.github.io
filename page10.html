Our Vision

Mission Statement: 

Fire International Embassy seeks to advance the kingdom of God in the beauties of holiness, by preaching the gospel to all people and making disciples of all men.

Vision statement 

Our Vision is to be a church where people of all denominations are welcome to worship God in truth and in the spirit. We seek to Free, ignite, revival and enthroned people all around the world by the preached word of the gospel

Core values 

Love- Unity -Humility- Integrity -Holiness -Peace
