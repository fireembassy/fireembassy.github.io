1 YEAR ANNIVERSARY POSPONED DUE DO COVID-19

On behalf of Fire International Embassy I'd like to inform everyone that the one year anniversary has been postponed due to Covid-19. As much as we'd like to keep our original dates the safety and well-being of all guest is priority and we plan to take advantage of this extra time to make the Anniversary even bigger & better. The event has been changed to June 25-28th . We are hoping these future dates give the opportunity to those that were not able to make it a chance to attend. If you have already purchased tickets for the banquet, they will be reissued with the correct dates. If you have not, tickets can now be purchased on our ministry website; fireinternationalembassy.org.
We do express our sincere apology for any inconveniences and pray everyone stays safe along with their families.
